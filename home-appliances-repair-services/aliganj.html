Common Air Conditioner Issues

Faulty AC bothering you? Try not to fiddle around and make it worse. Every appliance comes with a shelf life and with regular wear and tear, air conditioners performance or efficiency may deteriorate. Timely maintenance is essential be it AC cleaning, changing AC spare parts, filling up the gas etc. depending on the requirement. Technical issues are best handled by the experts but as users it is always advised to be aware of the basic AC issues and why it occurs. Listed below are some of the most common AC issues that warrant a professional AC repair expert to fix the same.

  • Air condition starting problem

    If you are facing AC troubleshooting problems, lack of power supply, a tripped circuit breaker could be a potential reason. In such cases, the circuit breaker must be rest or the blown fuse must be replaced. Thermostat issues, broken or loose wiring can also be possibly the reason.

  • Air condition not cooling enough

    One of the most obvious reasons that your AC is not blowing cold air is due to a lack of required airflow. Dirty air filter clogs the filter limiting the airflow over the evaporator coils. This could frost the evaporator affecting the airflow even more.

  • Power supply issues

    In the case of power supply issues, it is advised to reset the electrical switch. If the problem persists, you need to get a professional AC repair expert to check the indoor regulator and wiring.

  • Abnormal sound from the AC unit

    The AC unit makes squealing noise if the belt connecting o the motor has slipped. AC’s that don’t have a belt system should be replaced with a new belt. Malfunctioning of the AC, such as loose part, refrigerant leaks, broken isolation feet, etc. can also be potential reasons for the same.

  • Sudden breakdown of the AC

    Grimy air channels can be one of the reasons for unexpected AC breakdown. This restricts the wind stream. Limited wind stream can birth an array of issues such as solidified evaporator curl leading to on and off glitches.

  • AC coil problems

    Frozen indoor coils can be caused due to low refrigerant. Frozen coil indicates that there is an issue with the airflow as a result of filthy air or obstructed ductwork. There are various reasons that can hamper your AC condenser loop, which is vital to cool the air.

  • Fan blade issues

    The fan in the compressor unit does the job of conveying the heat from your house to the outside air. If the fan doesn’t function properly, there wouldn’t be proper heat transfer leading to overheating and damaging the compressor. The fan blades are made of metal, and any damage such as twist or breakage can harm and is unsafe for the framework. Poor fan engine functionality may cause a loud sound every time you use the AC.

  • Water spillage

    Every AC owner must have faced the problem of water spillage at least once. Water spillage from the indoor AC unit needs a quick fix. Generally, the indoor evaporator curls and the suction line tends to sweat, and so it is always kept enclosed in dark protection. Trickling can be caused if the protection layer is damaged and can be difficult to fix. Another reason can be that the evaporator expels abundant stickiness from the air and delivers a lot of water, which runs outside the curl into a dish with a channel. The channel might be directed to the drain or the ground.

Common Refrigerator Problems

Like other home electrical appliances, refrigerators also require cleaning and servicing for efficient working. Neglecting faults in beginning can lead to bigger problems and result in refrigeration replacement also. Regular cleaning, timely servicing, filling gas, etc. can help you get better performance. To resolve the technical problems of your fridge, you can book a refrigerator repair service. The experienced and skilled engineers would inspect your refrigerator and fix the issue. Here are some of the common refrigerator problems that are resolved by the technicians

  • Refrigerator Not Cooling

    No cooling or less cooling is one of the most common problems faced by refrigerator users. There can be numerous reasons behind this problem like

    • The condenser coils of the fridge get dirty due to which they do not work properly. Cleaning them thoroughly can resolve the issue.
    • The evaporator fan motor might not be working. Getting the motor repaired or replaced by an expert is an ideal way to get rid of this problem.
    • Fault in the start relay affects the functioning of the condenser. You would need a professional for the replacement or repair of the start relay.
    • Fault in temperature control thermostat can cut the supply voltage to the evaporator fan motor, compressor. For resolving this issue, you need professional help.
  • Refrigerator Not Defrosting

    Here is a list of reasons behind the defrosting issues in refrigerator

    • In case there is a defect in the defrost timer, the system might not send power to the defrost heater. This might require replacement
    • If the defrost sensor of the fridge blows off, the system would stop working. Resetting the defrost sensor fuse cannot help. Getting the sensor fused replaced by the technician is the only option.
    • Failure of the defrost control board can influence the defrost cycle. You would need professional help for the inspection and solution.
  • Water Leakage

    If your refrigerator is leaking water, there could be multiple reasons behind this problem such as

    • The problem is water tank assemble can also cause leakage.
    • In case the valves are loose, securing them tightly would help. However, if the inlet valves are cracked, you need to expert to replace them.
    • Cracked water filter head, drain pan, or water filter housing can cause water leakage. Getting the broken or torn parts replaced by technicians would help you.
  • Refrigerator Making Noise

    If your refrigerator is creating strange noises it could be due to the failure of the freezer’s evaporator fan motor. Along with this, another reason could be the faulty condenser blade. If the condenser blades are free from obstructions, they make noise. For diagnosing and resolving the actual reason behind this problem, you can take professional help.

  • Freezing Food

    The freezer freezes the food whereas the fresh food compartment of the refrigerator keeps the food cool. However, if you get ice crystals on the food in the fresh food compartment, there is some issue. The reason behind this problem could be a defect in the main control board, temperature control thermostat, and thermistor or temperature control board. Booking a reliable refrigerator repair service technician would be an ideal solution to resolve this issue.

  • Cool Freezer and Warm Fridge

    Many times, this happens when the freezer remains cool but the fresh food compartment stays warm. The possible reasons for this problem are

    • If the evaporator fan motor stops working, the cold air is not circulated to the fresh food compartment. This can be a reason for the fridge being warm.
    • Faulty defrost timer and control board can also affect the defrost cycle and refrigerators work.
    • Defect in the defrost heater assembly

Common Water Purifier Issues

The RO water purifier system is the best way to get clean and safe drinking water in today’s polluted world. Water purifiers will usually work without problems for many years provided if you maintain regular service and repair services. Periodic maintenance helps your water filter in good condition and have access to safe drinking water. The problem occurs when you don’t maintain the water filter system regularly. Lack of maintenance will cause water filtration operation issues, thus resulting in bad taste of water, receiving foul smell, or any other kind of technical issues. It is always best to hire experts to handle any kind of technical issues in your water purifier. Below are some general water purifier issues that you need to take expert’s help to get them fixed.

  • Bad water taste or foul smell in the water

    Stagnant water is the main reason of getting foul smell in the water. Depleted filters and worn-out membranes are the other major reasons why you get bad water taste from your purifier. For these, you will have to replace the depleted components so that you get safe drinking water with good taste. The water in the tank needs to get changed periodically so that air and bubbles are reduced and the water is safe to drink.

  • Leakage in faucet

    If there is any system parts left loosely fitted in your water purifier, then some amount of leakage occurs there. Try to identify where the leakage occurs and where it is exactly originating from. Sometimes, it will be from the bottom of the faucet stem. You can tighten all the fittings and push the tubing into the valves, drain saddle and ports. This will tighten the joint and avoid leakage. However, getting an expert’s help will solve the issue completely.

  • A noisy faucet

    A noisy faucet or drain is one of the most common issues that people experience in the RO water purifier system. This usually occurs when you install a new water purifier system or when you change the filter cartridges, and the air gets pushed out after it. There’s no need to worry about this problem much as the noise will gradually get reduced in a week. An additional thing that you could do is to set the tubes straight and check for gaps again in the system if the noise problem persists further. You can also seek help from experts through Sulekha to help you out. Regular water purifier service will increase the shelf life of your RO and provide safe drinking water, so make sure you do the maintenance service regularly.

  • Slow and low water flow

    Slow water flow than usual is another common problem related to pressure and when the volume of water is less. You have to check if the water pressure is optimal in the RO tank. If it’s not normal, then raise the value to 6 – 8 psi by using a pressure gauge carefully. Sometimes, the bladder inside the water tank cannot hold the pressure and in that case, the whole tank should be replaced.

  • Vibrations in Water Purifier

    While there is a change in the filter in your water purifier, this problem occurs. This is due to pressure differential vibrations. The difference in pressure between the different filters causes vibrations in the purifier. However, this doesn’t cause much damage to your purifier and will generally fade away within 3-4 days.

    Repairing an RO system yourself can be a tedious process as opening and reassembling the entire system for problems would take a lot of time and at times it will end up in mess. So, you can get help from water purifier experts through Sulekha at any time at your affordable costs. Feel free from worrying in the comfort of your own home!

Common Microwave Issues

If your microwave is acting out of order and you don’t know what to do, we advise you to not do anything. Issues with electrical appliances might worsen if you try to fix it without knowing the right procedure. Sulekha has listed verified and experienced professionals in the field of microwave repair and technicians. Periodic check from experts and quick response to electrical issues will help you extend the shelf life of you microwave oven. As microwave owner, you should know a few things about handling and fixing microwaves.

  • Microwave does not heat

    Microwave not heating is a common problem, and the most popular reason behind it is magnetron failure. A magnetron uses high voltage to produce microwave frequency to cook food. If the microwave is turned on when it is empty, this causes the magnetron to burn out. A burned magnetron has to be replaced as it cannot be repaired. Other probable causes behind this problem are faulty diodes, capacitor burn, transformer failure, and defective door switch.

  • Microwave runs and then stops

    There can be many causes behind this issue, and only through a thorough inspection, one can come to a conclusion. A few common reasons for a microwave oven to turn on and stop immediately are faulty door switch, failed transformer, defective touchpad and faulty fan motor.

  • Microwave buttons do not work

    If the microwave buttons do not work, it indicates that the touchpad or the control panel are defective or have been damaged. Another possible reason can be the faulty main control board (motherboard).

  • Microwave plate does not spin

    In a microwave, there is a rotating motor below the plate which is responsible for the rotation of the spinning plate. So the most probable cause behind this issue is a worn out or burned motor. Motor can be easily replaced. However, if the motor is not defective this indicates a fault in the main control board. If the control board is fine, then check the touchpad or control panel for the problem.

  • Microwave light-bulb does not turn on during operation

    There is a small bulb inside your microwave located behind the grill, which lights when the microwave runs. If the bulb does not work, then it may have fused. Another possible reasons are faulty bulb socket and bad wiring. If the bulb, socket, and wiring are fine then check the main control board.

Common Kitchen Chimney Issues

A chimney is not a low-maintenance appliance like many people think of it. A costly yet striking kitchen chimney means taking additional responsibilities. To ensure that the heat produced in the kitchen doesn’t start a house fire or in any other way endanger your home and family, a chimney inspection is critical. Some common chimney problems and fixes are: 

  • Blockage

    Chimney obstruction or blockage is caused because of various factors, including obstruction of the chimney draft. If a chimney doesn’t have sufficient height, it can prevent smoke from going out of the Chimney the way it’s supposed to. Less air pressure inside than outside, caused by a house that’s too airtight, can sometimes draw the smoke into the home. Chimney blockage is dangerous; it can cause toxic fumes such as deadly carbon monoxide to enter the home. Connect with our experts immediately if the problem persists.

  • Build up in the Chimney

    Over time, the kitchen chimney collects creosote that gets deposited in the process of cooking. Creosote is a mixture of black or brown tar and soot, which is a fine carbon powder. Creosote formation occurs stage-wise, and it’s highly recommended to have chimney inspections annually. Thousands of house fires caused by dirty chimneys occur every year, and it is preventable.

  • Unwanted Noise

    Noise is a very common problem in electric Chimney. Higher motor speed means more sound. But many times the reason behind excessive noise can be something else. If you did not do the cleaning of your kitchen chimney for a long time, then that may create malfunctioning of Chimney or excessive noise, or obstruction in the motor fan can also create unwanted noise.

  • Suction not working

    Kitchen chimneys absorb oily & spicy fume from your kitchen and then filter that fume and send filtered air outside. If the suction system is not working, that means it is not performing its actual task. Such a major issue may happen due to a damaged motor.

  • The fan is rotating slowly

    Sometime suction is okay, but not properly working as the fan is rotating very slowly, so the kitchen chimney is not being able to apply maximum suction power. This problem may be rectified by changing the condenser (capacitor) or servicing the motor (greasing, replacing damaged ball bearing, etc.).

  • Electric Chimney not running

    If your electric kitchen chimney is not getting any power supply, then you must check power at switch board and visually inspect the power cable to find any damaged area. If everything is in place and the switchboard is also working properly, then connect with Sulekha’s chimney repair experts as it may be due to issues in the internal circuit.

Common Water Dispenser Issues

  • Water Leakage

    Usually, first-time users get confused between an overflowing drip and a leaking water cooler. In offices, the water dispenser is used by many people and there are chances that they dispose of the water in the drip tray. You can empty the drip tray to avoid spillage and check if there is leakage.

    The reason behind the leakage could be the improper closing of the back valve of the water cooler. You can check that part and ensure if that is well-sealed. Even after this if you experience water leakage, you need to contact a water dispenser service expert.

  • Water Tastes and Smells Bad

    It is said that water is pure if it is tasteless, odourless, and colourless. If you experience odour and sudden change in the taste of water, then there is an issue.   • Broken Tap

    With the pressure and rough and tough use, the tap of the water dispenser might get crack. Ignoring the cracks at the initial stage might result in the breakage of the tap. For replacing the broken tap, you can take the help of a technician.

  • Water is not Hot

    In case the water dispenser is not pouring hot water, the hot tank might be off. You can check the switch and ensure if it is in the correct position. Common Water Heater and Geyser Issues

There are several issues that are commonly faced by owners of geysers and water heaters. Tinkering with water heaters and geysers on your own is not a good idea, as it could end up damaging the appliance. So, here are the most common geyser and water heater issues:

  • Geyser Overheating

    Geyser overheating is something that is still very common, in spite of many modern-day geysers and water heaters featuring auto cut-off features. In case the auto cut-off feature is hampered due to technical difficulties, you may see steam along with hot water come out of the water heater.

    Damaged, defective, and faulty thermostats are often the cause of geyser overheating. However, you should always get it checked by a professional to ensure that there are no other underlying causes. Delaying repairs for an overheating geyser may lead to severe hazards such as the geyser exploding.

  • Constant Dripping

    All geysers have overflow pipes connected to the pressure control valves. Occasionally, you should see some water dripping out of these overflow pipes. However, if the occasional turns into something far more frequent and regular, then it’s a cause for concern.

    Immediately get in touch with the Sulekha-listed water heater and geyser repair experts as continuous water-dripping may indicate a control valve that needs to be repaired. In the worst case, you may have to purchase a new control valve to replace the old one. You can count on the Sulekha-listed services for both repairs and replacements.

  • Damaged Light Indicators

    Geysers have different light indicators to signal when they are heating water (typically green) and when they have heated the maximum capacity of water (typically red). Sometimes, internal wear and tear lead to these indicators malfunctioning, which can make it hard for you to tell when the geyser is heating water and when it is done.

    Simply get in touch with a geyser and water heater repair expert on Sulekha to have this common geyser problem resolved in next to no time.

  • Lack of Hot Water after Heating

    So you left your geyser on for around 15-20 minutes, expecting it to give you piping hot water for a much-needed shower. However, after 20 minutes, the geyser has no hot water, even though the ‘heating’ light indicator shows you otherwise. In such a situation, the problem may lie with the heating element inside the geyser.

    To fix your heating element, you should immediately reach out to a Sulekha-listed repair expert. A professional can tell you whether the existing heating element can be repaired or if it has to be replaced.

  • Hot Water Pressure Not High Enough

    If the hot water pressure feels lacking when you are taking a shower, there may be a sedimentation problem within the tank of the geyser. To free the tank from sediments, you need professional help, which you can find easily through us at Sulekha.

    Our listed experts can check for corrosion, blockages, and rust build-up inside the tank and get rid of all obstructions to ensure that the hot water flow and pressure are high.
