Hiring Guide for Maid Service in Mumbai

Life in cities is always hard-pressed with people being involved in so many activities from day till night. And even in a bustling city like Mumbai it's all action-packed. Right from children going to school to people leaving to work every morning it is all hassles. And it doesn't stop there people have lots of other things to do after work till they go to sleep. Whether it is male or female it doesn't matter each one in a family has their own responsibilities to do. The household chores seem to be a big burden that will leave you in awe and frustrated.

Expert maids work hard to keep your house sparkling clean your food deliciously made your senior citizens well looked after and your babies giggling gleefully. They use their time skillfully and methodically to complete their work efficiently. To maintain their reputation they keep up their professionalism and keep up a good rapport with their employers. The maid's charges vary according to their services.

Types of maid services offered in Mumbai

The types of services you can expect from maids are

  • Dusting: Dust accumulates quickly on the sofa, table and appliances life TV, microwave, refrigerator, etc. Moreover, it is difficult to spare the time for cleaning the corners or different areas of the house like behind the bed and sofa. The maid would clean the appliances, picture frames, artworks. Etc and prevent them from dirt and dust.
  • Cleaning the House: Sweeping and mopping the floors are common tasks that ensure the cleanliness of the house.
  • Handling Clothes and Related Tasks: You can hire the maids for the washing as well as ironing of clothes, changing linens, making the bed.
  • Kitchen Management: Washing the dishes, arranging the cupboards, cleaning the kitchen appliances and countertops, etc are the tasks that you can expect from a maid.
  • Housekeeping: The maids will take care of sweeping. mopping, the floor dusting, washing, drying and folding the clothes and washing the vessels. Rest assured they will take care of all the chores successfully.
  • Cooking: You have to provide them with the grocery and condiments and they will surely prepare you a delicious and satisfying meal. One less thing you would have to worry about in your daily routine.
  • Babysitting: They are well-trained to take care of any tantrum the baby throws and handle it properly. Maids will feed the baby change their diapers and keep them entertained. They give great insights into raising a child and they also make it a point to keep everything hygienic around the baby.
  • Senior Citizen Caretaking: Maids hired to look after senior citizens need to be well-versed in handling medical equipment and medication. They will be able to handle the senior citizen if they lose balance or consciousness. They are certified by reputed institutions to take care of senior citizens.

There are few things that you should take into account before you finalize a maid in the safety and trust aspect.

  • Check the credibility of the maid agency and ask if they do proper background checks before sending any maid
  • Check if the maid service fits in your budget. You can compare the cost of different service providers and finalize them

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What does a Maid do?

A maid can do all the household chores, including cooking, cleaning, dusting, mopping the floor and washing dishes. You can hire maids for specific tasks as well; these specific tasks include Babysitting, older person care, cooking, cleaning etc. There are maids who also does all the above for people on full time and part-time basis

2. Are maids given training?

If you are hiring a maid from a placement agency you can rest assured that they are trained to do their work perfectly.

3. Can I hire maids for my office?

Yes you can. There are agencies on Sulekha listings which offer services to offices.

4. What happens in case a maid quits?

If a maid quits before their stipulated time the agency from where you hired them will appoint a replacement maid in their place.

5. What is the monthly salary of maids in Mumbai?

A full-time maid's salary is between Rs. 15 000 to Rs. 30 000 per month and for part-timers the salary would range from Rs. 5 000 to Rs. 7 000.

6. What services can I expect from a professional maid?

You can choose any or a combination of services from the below:

  • Part-time, full-time domestic help to get your regular household chores done like cleaning, organizing kitchen utensils, mopping, washing clothes, cooking etc.
  • Patient care for those who require constant care and help
  • Babysitter services
  • Cook services would help you with properly cooked meals customized according to your taste and health restrictions

7. How do I know if I should hire a full time or part time maid?

You should consider hiring a maid depending on the need and your budget. The longer the service hours, the higher they charge.

  • Part-time maids provide assistance for 4-5 hours to complete the daily chores like cleaning, cooking, washing vessels etc.
  • You should consider hiring a full-time maid when you constantly require help for various household functions right from completing daily chores to taking care of elderly people or your kids.
  • Full-time worker provides services throughout the day and usually stays within your residence. Full-time house help is generally provided with basic amenities of food and shelter facilities.

8. How much does it cost to hire a full-time maid in Mumbai?

Service providers charge based on the work requirements, duration of service and the number of family members. Generally, full-time maids charge somewhere starting from 12,000 and may go up to 15,000/20,000 depending upon the work requirements. Agencies would schedule an interview for you with a potential maid to discuss your requirements, things to provide the maid while on service and salary details to proceed accordingly

9. Do I have to be at home while the maid comes for work?

Not really. Since you have all her background details you can relax and allow the maid to work at her own convenience. This applies to both part-time maids as well as full-time maids.

Sulekha allows users to go through all the offline Maid service provider agencies of your city on its portal. It becomes very easy for users to get access to multiple service provider and allows for a competitive salary for the maid as well as the user.
